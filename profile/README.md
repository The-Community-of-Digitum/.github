# The Republic of Digitum

Welcome to the official GitHub home of **The Republic of Digitum** — a tiny digital nation built on creativity, PCGs (Protected Cultural Goods), and the sacred art of Deep‑Fried Bread.

Digitum is a place where:
- Recipes are **cultural artifacts**
- Holidays are **monthly rituals**
- Code and cuisine coexist
- The Founder’s cravings shape the calendar

---

## 🥖 National Deep‑Fried Bread Day
Digitum’s signature holiday, celebrated on the **1st of every month**.

On this day:
- A Deep‑Fried Bread recipe is posted publicly  
- Citizens copy it, cook it, and celebrate  
- The recipe is deleted the next day (as required by PCG law)

### ⭐ Bonus Holiday
**June 10** — *The First Fry*  
The spontaneous, canonically recognized pre‑launch celebration.

This organization hosts:
